NOFX is an open-source AI trading operating system designed for quantitative trading across US stocks, commodities, forex, and crypto markets. It connects multiple exchanges and pits diverse AI models against each other in competitive strategy evaluation, forming a self-evolving pipeline from research to live deployment.

## Multi-Exchange Connectivity

- Multi-exchange adapters provide unified connectivity for order execution and fund management across platforms like Binance and Hyperliquid
- Built-in historical backtesting engine and real-time data stream processing pipelines for strategy validation
- Microservices architecture with containerized deployment for horizontal scalability and high availability

## Multi-Model Competition

- Multi-agent competition enables parallel strategy evaluation, model selection, and ensemble decision-making
- Supports heterogeneous models such as DeepSeek, Qwen, and Claude for comparative evaluation
- Unified integration layer for model orchestration and evaluation across trading strategies

## Self-Evolving Pipelines

- Integrates model comparison, online evaluation, and automated strategy updates in a continuous loop
- Real-time visual dashboards and alerting systems provide full visibility into strategy performance
- Released under AGPL-3.0 license enabling community collaboration and full auditability for institutional compliance
