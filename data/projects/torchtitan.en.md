## Overview

TorchTitan provides a production-ready PyTorch-native solution for LLM pretraining, offering multi-dimensional parallelism and practical training recipes.

## Key features

- FSDP2 and various parallelism strategies.
- Training recipes and deployment guidance.

## Use cases

- Pretraining large generative models and distributed experiments.

## Technical highlights

- Close integration with PyTorch distributed primitives and tooling.
