## Overview

Sympozium is the coordination layer for multi-agent AI systems. It provides the orchestration and communication infrastructure needed for multiple AI agents to work together effectively on complex tasks.

## Key Features

- Multi-agent coordination and orchestration
- Agent communication infrastructure
- Task distribution and result aggregation
- Designed for complex, multi-step AI workflows

## Use Cases

- Orchestrating multiple specialized AI agents on complex tasks
- Building multi-agent pipelines with role-based agent teams
- Enterprise AI workflow coordination

## Technical Details

- MIT licensed, open-source coordination layer
- Agentic AI architecture
- Supports multi-agent communication patterns
