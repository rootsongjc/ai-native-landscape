## Overview

Obsidian Second Brain is a cross-CLI skill that transforms an Obsidian vault into an AI-powered second brain. It integrates with Claude Code and other AI agents to enable intelligent note search, summarization, linking, and knowledge management directly from the terminal.

## Key Features

- Cross-CLI integration with Claude Code and other AI coding agents.
- Turns Obsidian vaults into queryable AI knowledge bases.
- Intelligent search, summarization, and linking across notes.
- MIT licensed and extensible.

## Use Cases

- Query and synthesize knowledge from your Obsidian vault via AI agents.
- Automate note linking and knowledge graph construction.
- Use AI to surface relevant notes during coding and research sessions.
